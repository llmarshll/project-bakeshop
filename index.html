    About Us

Conti's Bake Shop's Famous Chef

Our very own, Chef Christian Anthony Castillo. A Baker, Cook, Garde Manger, Teacher, and Occasional Traveler. Chef Consultant at 1028 Kitchen Place. Studied Master in Business Administration Major in Hotel and Restaurant Management at La Consolacion University. Studied at Bulacan State University.

Our Staffs, Crew, and Managers

Since opening its doors to families and friends in Paranaque in 1997. Conti's Bakeshop has created and shared memories of togetherness to people who have come to love our wide selection of products, including our signature dishes such as the Baked Salmon, Chicken Pie, and the well loved Mango Bravo. We are committed to make every guest experience memorable and to continue sharing our story in every product we create and serve.
